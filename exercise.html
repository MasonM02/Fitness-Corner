Simple Exercises for each Body Part!

Barbell Bench Press

Sit down on the bench, make sure pinkies are lined up with lines on bar, make sure feet do not slip when u push down on them, and then lifting the bar and bring it down to your chest, at the top of your pectoral muscle, then slowly push it back up to starting position and repeat.

Cable Tricep Pushdown

Sit down on the bench, make sure pinkies are lined up with lines on bar, make sure feet do not slip when u push down on them, and then lifting the bar and bring it down to your chest, at the top of your pectoral muscle, then slowly push it back up to starting position and repeat.

Dumbbell Alternating Curls

Stand straight up with a dumbbell in each hand. Bring one dumbbell up, not moving your upper arm only your lower half of your arm, hold at the top for a second then release and bring the dumbbell down. After this is done repeat with the opposite arm. Completing one rep of each arm counts as one total rep with this exercise.

Lateral Raises

Stand up straight with a dumbbell in each arm at your side. Puff out your chest and bring up your arms parallel to your body with a slight bend in your elbow, bringing them up to about shoulder height or a little below. Then bring them down slowly and repeat.

Barbell Bent Over Row

Bend slightly over with your back straightened. Now lift the barbell from your knees to your lower chest/abdomen in a backward pulling motion, letting your elbows be tucked into your back, then release and go back down and repeat.

Squat(Barbell Squat)

This is a body weight exercise but can be enhanced by adding a barbell to it. Standing straight up, you bend your knees and stick your butt out. Knees at a 90 degree angle with your weight on your feet. Then rise back to full height and repeat. If you feel like body weight is not doing enough you can put a barbell on your back and do the same thing with the barbell attached.
