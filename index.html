One of the most basic aspects of lifting is the terminology. Most movements/things you do in the gym have a name to them. For example, the hierarchy of lifting goes rep, set, exercises, workout. Think of it like a Russian doll where each term is within the next and so on. A workout has 4-8 exercises within it, and each exercise has 2-6 sets within it, and each set has 2-15 reps within it. So it goes Workout->Exercise->Set->Rep!
